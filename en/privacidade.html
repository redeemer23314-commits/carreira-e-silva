2. What data we collect

2.1. Data you provide in the quote request form

When you fill in the quote request form, we collect the data you enter there, namely:

  • Contact details: name, email address, telephone/WhatsApp and postcode;
  • Details about the move: type of move, origin and destination addresses, floors, whether there is a lift, description of the items to be transported, special items (e.g. piano, safe), additional services required, preferred date and date flexibility;
  • Message: any information you choose to include in the notes field.

Please do not include sensitive data. We ask that you do not write unnecessary or sensitive information in the form (for example, health data, religious or political beliefs, or bank details). For the quote we only need information relating to the move.

2.2. Data collected automatically

  • IP address and date/time of the request: recorded in the server's technical logs and used by the security mechanisms that limit the number of form submissions, in order to prevent spam and abuse.

2.3. Volume calculator

The volume estimate calculator available on the contact page runs entirely in your browser. The items you select are not sent to or stored on our servers, unless you write them into the quote request form.

6. Cookies and similar technologies

This website does not use advertising, analytics or user-tracking cookies. We do not use tools such as Google Analytics or social media pixels.

Fonts: the fonts used on this website are hosted on our own servers. Unlike what happens when services such as Google Fonts are used, viewing the site does not involve any connection to third-party servers nor the disclosure of your IP address to Google.

You should, however, be aware of the following:

  • Google Maps: the home page includes a Google map that is not loaded automatically. A notice is shown in place of the map, and no data is sent to Google until you click the "Load map" button. If you choose to load it, Google may receive your IP address and set its own cookies, under Google's policies. If you never click the button, no contact whatsoever is made with Google.
  • Restricted area: the administration page, intended exclusively for authorised staff, uses the browser's local storage (localStorage) to keep the session open. This is a technology strictly necessary for that area to work and is not used on the public pages of the website.

8. Data security

We adopt appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss or destruction, namely: encrypted communication between your browser and our servers (HTTPS), access to the request database restricted to authorised staff subject to authentication, validation and filtering of the content submitted in the form, and limits on the number of submissions per user.

9. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: to know what data of yours we process and obtain a copy;
  • Rectification: to correct inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten"): to request the deletion of your data, where applicable;
  • Restriction: to request the suspension of processing in certain situations;
  • Portability: to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format;
  • Objection: to object to processing based on our legitimate interests;
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time, where processing is based on it, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out beforehand.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at geral@carreiraesilva.pt. We may request additional information to confirm your identity. We will reply within a maximum of one month, extendable as provided by law.

If you consider that the processing of your data infringes applicable law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the national supervisory authority: Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados (CNPD) — www.cnpd.pt.

10. Automated decision-making

We do not make decisions based solely on automated processing that produce legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affect you. The volume estimate and the automatic validation of the form are merely instrumental and do not replace human review of your request.

11. Changes to this Policy

This Policy may be updated whenever necessary, in particular due to legal changes or changes to the services provided. The version in force is always the one published on this page, stating the date on which it was last updated.
