Carreira & Silva

A family business since 1998.

Founded in 1998 by the couple Jorge Carreira and Laurinda Silva, Carreira & Silva was born when Jorge returned from France to Portugal with the goal of building a moving company — its very first job being the move of his own family's belongings.

What began as a family project is today a company spanning two generations, which in recent years has seen remarkable growth, even doubling its turnover. Initially specialised in removals, it has expanded into transport, logistics and storage, with the same dedication as on day one.

About the group

A trusted company with over 25 years of experience.

Carreira & Silva was born out of the need to move without the usual chaos: uncertain deadlines, damaged or lost goods, lack of information. We built our way of working around a simple principle — everything that is yours must be identified, protected and accounted for from start to finish.

Over the years we have gained experience in residential and business moves, transport of large furniture and self-storage management, always with the same commitment: caring for each item as if it were our own.

We work with the right equipment, appropriate protective materials and teams with hands-on experience in delicate items and hard-to-reach spaces.
